Flying
  
glowing
  
zipping
  
hiding
  
the little bird opens it's beak
  
feeding her babies
  
tiny and small
  
cute
  
resting in the twig nest
  
feeding on worms
  
slimy
  
squirmy
  
flashing red
  
a cardinal.
