Sweet
  
juicy
  
yummy
  
strawberries
  
eat them up!
  
More better news!
  
Time to go strawberry picking!
  
We ride on the bumpy road
  
Just cannot sit still
  
with such excitement!
  
Finally!
  
We're there!
  
Plucking the strawberries,
  
They're so good!
  
Save them for chocolate
  
dipping,
  
When we reach home
  
Chocolate
  
dipping is the
  
best of all!
