The snow falls
  
It touches the ground
  
Like angels
  
being sent from god
  
I open my mouth
  
The cool snow touches my tongue
  
It melts away
  
I shiver
  
The snow is cold
  
Brrr
  
It's time for me to come inside
  
My mother makes hot chocolate
  
I sip it
  
Warm and happyz
