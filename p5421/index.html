Brilliant Blue

Jun 10, 2014

My color

My favorite

popular

Blue

rarely I see a car

like

the blue car

over there

so popular

Everyone loves blue

any blue color

as long as it is blue

dark blue color

and light blue

is the most popular

Blue, blue, and more blue

Shining blue car

Sparkling blue

A blur

of all blue
