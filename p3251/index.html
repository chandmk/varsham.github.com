Feb 01, 2014

Anna was at the top of the tree but she saw her friends. In a flash, she was being cornered by ice controls.
"What are these? " asked Anna. "Your teacher wanted you to figure out! ". Anna made an ice flower and threw it at the ice controls.
Anna backed away as on moved closer to her. Anna dodged the ice tacks and made an ice mountain. The HORRIBLE ICE CONTROL.
