Poems are Made

Poems are made because of the ideas

Poems are made to remember the forgotten things

Poems are made to burst out your happiness and make peace

Poems are made to stop the miserable things that come in your heart

Poems are made because of the wonderful nature which can be loved and shared

Poems are made to encourage and inspire children like me who are hungry for writing
