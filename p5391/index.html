Bird

  • Jun 10, 2014
  • Categories: Uncategorized
  • 1 min read

Flying

glowing

zipping

hiding

the little bird opens it’s beak

feeding her babies

tiny and small

cute

resting in the twig nest

feeding on worms

slimy

squirmy

flashing red

a cardinal.
