The Hypnocycle

  • Jun 9, 2013
  • Categories: poem
  • 1 min read

You never got yourself straight on a hypnocycle,

Which feels like a bed,

That ol’e hypnocycle will doze your self to sleep,

Oh Joe, please don’t ride that again.
