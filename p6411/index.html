Mother

Oh! Of all

The mothers,

Have you known, that you are the best?

Extrodinary mother

Running with magic like no other

Father

**A ** hard worker, in what way can I

Thank him

He works so hard

Extrodinary

Running with the magic of love

Sister

**I ** love her

She is a little sister’s best friend

Thank her, because of her

Extrodinary help

Running with magic of friendship
